5. Appendix

Screen coodinates \( \vv \in \mathbb{R}^2 \) to the points on the unit sphere \( \vp \in \mathbb{R}^3 ,\, |\vp| = 1 \) :

Azimuthal equidistant (My proposal) $$ \block{align}{ (p_1,\, p_2) &= \sin \L( \frac{\pi}{2} |\vv| \R) \, \frac{\vv}{|\vv|} \\ p_3 &= \cos \L( \frac{\pi}{2} |\vv| \R) } $$
Orthographic (Shoemake) $$ \block{align}{ (p_1,\, p_2) &= \vv \\ p_3 &= \sqrt{1 - {|\vv|}^2} && \if {|\vv|}^2 < 1, \\ \\ (p_1,\, p_2) &= \frac{\vv}{|\vv|} \\ p_3 &= 0 && \otherwise. } $$
Orthographic + hyperbola (Holroyd) $$ \block{align}{ (p_1, \, p_2) &= \vv \\ p_3 &= \sqrt{1 - {|\vv|}^2} && \if {|\vv|}^2 < \frac{1}{2}, \\ \\ (\tilde{p}_1,\, \tilde{p}_2) &:= \vv \\ \tilde{p}_3 &:= \frac{1}{2 |\vv|} \\ \vp &= \frac{\vec{\tilde{p}}}{|\vec{\tilde{p}}|} && \otherwise. } $$

Point pairs \( \vp_0, \ \vp_1 \) on the unit sphere to the rotation \( \qq \) :

$$ \cos \theta := {\vp_0} \cdot {\vp_1} \,,\, \vec{a} := \frac{ \vp_0 \times \vp_1 }{ | \vp_0 \times \vp_1 | } $$

1×, path dependent $$ \qq = \cos \frac{\theta}{2} + \boldsymbol{a} \sin \frac{\theta}{2} $$
2×, path independent (Shoemake) $$ \qq = \cos \theta + \boldsymbol{a} \sin \theta = \qp_1 \, \bar{\qp}_0 $$
